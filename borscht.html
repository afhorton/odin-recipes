Borscht

Description:

Borscht is the quintessential Ukrainian dish. A common expression in Ukraine is

"If there is no borscht in the house, there is nothing to eat."

Ingredients:

  • Beats
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cabbage

Steps:

  1. Fry the beats
  2. Stir in the tomato sauce
  3. Cut the cabbage
  4. Put it all together
